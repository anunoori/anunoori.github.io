About The Lakeside Leo

Volume I, 2020-2021

The Lakeside Leo was founded by Anushka Noori in 2020 as the student newspaper of Lakeside Middle School. The mission of The Leo was to foster community connection, social responsibility, and civic engagement. To fulfill this charge, Anushka assembled a team of over 50 student reporters, photographers, artists, creative writers, and editors.

The issues highlighted here comprise Volume I of The Leo - encompassing 170+ articles across 80+ pages - and were all published during Anushka's tenure as Editor-In-Chief of The Leo from 2020-2021.
